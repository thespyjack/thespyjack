About Our Institute

A Bridge Between Cultures

Founded in 2010, the Japanese Language Institute of Poland is the leading center for Japanese language education in Poland. Our mission is to promote Japanese language proficiency and cultural understanding between Poland and Japan.

With experienced native Japanese teachers and Polish educators specialized in Japanese language, we offer comprehensive programs that combine linguistic skills with cultural immersion.
